## Full Description (500 characters)
Drift is the sleep tracker that helps you understand and improve your sleep with AI-powered insights.

KEY FEATURES:
• Automatic sleep tracking via HealthKit
• AI sleep score with personalized insights
• Sleep debt tracking and recommendations
• Deep sleep, REM, and light sleep analysis
• Best sleep window detection
• Family sleep tracking (share with loved ones)
• Snoring and breathing regularity assessment
• Weekly sleep reports with trends
• Apple Watch companion app

PREMIUM FEATURES:
• 90-day sleep history
• Advanced AI correlations (exercise, caffeine, stress)
• Personalized sleep coaching
• Export sleep data
