## Tagline
**"Sleep smarter, live better."**

---

## Description

DriftMac brings intelligent sleep tracking to your Mac. Connect with Apple Health to monitor your sleep stages, track sleep debt, visualize trends, and wake up knowing how well you slept.

### Features

**Sleep Dashboard**
- Current sleep phase detection (Deep, REM, Light, Awake)
- Real-time sleep quality score
- Menu bar widget for at-a-glance insights

**Sleep Trends**
- 30 / 60 / 90 day trend analysis
- Personal baseline comparison
- Deep sleep, total sleep, and score tracking

**Sleep Debt Tracking**
- Weekly sleep debt calculation
- Recovery plan with actionable suggestions
- Visual chart of your last 7 nights

**Sleep Goals**
- Personalized daily sleep targets
- Progress toward weekly goals
- Reminders to hit your target

**Design**
- Dark purple aesthetic optimized for nighttime use
- Apple HealthKit integration
- Native macOS experience
